§ 01 — Brand Essence

A pocket for your terminal.
Built for control.

Pocket-T is the calm, fast, operator-grade layer that puts terminals, servers, and AI command tools within a thumb's reach — without ever feeling like a compromise.
Modern. Precise. Operator-grade.
The control plane in your pocket — built for the people who actually ship the work.
P/01

Precise

Every pixel, line, and label serves clarity. We strip the decorative; we sharpen the meaningful. If it isn't useful, it isn't there.

P/02

Reliable

Built for uptime, security, and confidence. The brand should feel as stable as the sessions it carries — never noisy, never anxious.

P/03

Fast

Optimized for speed and keyboard workflows. Type-first. Tap-second. Latency, both visual and literal, is the enemy.

P/04

Anywhere

Browser-native, mobile-first, friction-zero. The brand reads at 32 × 32 pixels and at 32 meters tall. No exceptions.

§ 03 — Construction

Clear space, sizing,
and minimum scale.

Clear space protects the mark from clutter and preserves its impact. X equals the cap-height of the "T".
>_Pocket-T
Minimum size · Digital
>_Pocket-T
120 px width · 24 px cap-height
Minimum size · Print
>_Pocket-T
20 mm width · 4 mm cap-height
Alignment

The >_ prompt always sits left of the wordmark, baseline-aligned. Never above, below, or trailing.

Spacing

Gap between prompt and wordmark equals 0.45 × cap-height. Optical centering trumps numeric exactness.

Weight

Wordmark locks to Space Grotesk SemiBold (600). Never Medium, Bold, or any italic.

Background

Prefer Off-White or Deep Graphite. On mid-tone or photographic backgrounds, use the reversed lockup with a 24% scrim.

§ 11 — Voice & Tone

How Pocket-T sounds.

We talk like a senior engineer who respects your time. Direct, precise, never patronizing. Confidence without volume.

We say

TaglineYour terminal, anywhere.
HeroControl everything. From anywhere.
OnboardingConnect a host. We'll handle the rest.
Empty stateNo sessions yet. Try ⌘K to jump to a host.
SuccessDeployed. 6 hosts in 12.4 s.
ErrorLost the connection at 10.0.0.42. Retrying.

We don't say

TaglineThe world's #1 AI-powered terminal experience.
HeroUnleash the power of remote computing!
OnboardingWelcome aboard! Let's get you set up. 🚀
Empty stateLooks like there's nothing here yet… 👀
Success🎉 Awesome! Deploy succeeded!
ErrorOops! Something went wrong. 😢
Direct

Lead with the verb. Cut the throat-clearing. Engineers don't have time for "We are excited to announce…"

Precise

Real numbers, real names, real units. "24 ms" beats "fast." "12 hosts" beats "many."

Calm

No exclamation marks in product. No emojis in chrome. No surprises in error states.

Operator-grade

We assume our user is smart, busy, and pays attention. We earn their trust by being worth their attention.

§ 12 — Motion & Imagery

Movement with intent.

Motion confirms state, never decorates. Imagery favors device, surface, and operator hand over abstract metaphors.
Motion principles
  • 120 ms Hover & focus. The smallest acknowledgements.
  • 180 ms Standard transitions. Sheets, drawers, popovers.
  • 280 ms Page-level. Route changes, splash dismissal.
  • EASE cubic-bezier(.2, .8, .2, 1) — the only curve we ship.
  • PULSE Live state — 1.6 s breath, 12% scale, mint glow.
Live state pulse

SYNCING

Imagery — yes
  • · Hands on device, top-down, neutral background
  • · Single device shots, Off-White or Graphite ground
  • · Real UI in real situations — café, train, desk
  • · Wide letterboxing, generous negative space
  • · Color story: graphite, mint accent, daylight
Imagery — no
  • · Stock "developer at computer" photography
  • · Saturated gradients, glowing 3D abstracts
  • · AI-generated humans or skin
  • · Cluttered desk scenes, busy backgrounds
  • · Heroes of a server rack or stock cityscape
